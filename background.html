My Background

Currently Arizonan by location, still Californian by heart. My grandfather worked with computers since my mother was a child, back in the days when cellphones were briefcases (and my grandfather even had one). I spent summers as a child in his office, surrounded by half demolished motherboards.

Developer Dreams

I love solving problems which is common for coders. I've seen many others talk about wnjoying seeing what they've built, and I enjoy that too, but in truth what makes me happiest is fixing what someone else built and is now broken. There's something uniquely satisying about walking into a messy room and leaving it absolutely perfect. My absolute favorite kind of logic puzzle.
